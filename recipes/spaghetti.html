Spaghetti al Formaggio. To dine or not to dine?

I must say this is defintely my favorite recipe. There's nothing better than Spaghetti al Formaggio and glass of wine during winter.

It's an easy and quick recipe that brings alot flavor to the table. Well, there's probably better dishes out there, but I'm a simple man with a simple taste. If you haven't tried this recipe before, please, give it a try!

List of Igredients - Cheese

  • Parmigiano Reggiano
  • Gorgonzola
  • Provolone
  • Taleggio

List of Igredients - Seasoning

  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • Milk and Cream

Step by Step - Preparation

  1. Combine milk and cream

  2. Melt in semi soft cheeses

  3. Add grated cheese (Parmesan)

  4. Add Seasoning

  5. Stir in pasta
