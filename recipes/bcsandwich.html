BaycanCheese - The Forbidden Sub

I know what you're thinking right now. Easy recipe huh? Definetely it is, and this is one isn't special in anyway.

The thing is: Some people are unaware of this delicasse. Outrageous, right? Since that is the case, I'm here to show you how to do it!

List of Igredients

  • Bacon
  • Cheese of your preference
  • Butter
  • Bread
  • Black Pepper
  • Eggs (Optional)

Step by Step - Preparation

  1. Fry the bacon and let it rest for some minutes
  2. On a griddle, toast your bread sandwiches with a little bit of butter
  3. Put some cheese and bacon between your toasted bread
  4. If you want eggs in your sandwich, just make a hole in your bread with a water glass and put the eggs inside
  5. Put you sanwich inside a pan so the cheese cant melt and the egg can fry

That's all folks
